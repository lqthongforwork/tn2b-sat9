🌤️ Tomorrow's Feelings Forecast

📝The exam is hard — she will feel nervous.

🧱 How we build it

Subject+will+verb

People will speak English. 🗣️

Subject+won't+verb

Robots won't do our homework. 🤖

Will+subject+verb?

Will you be happy? — Yes, I will. / No, I won't. ✅❌

👍 Yes (affirmative)I'll be relaxed. They will come.
👎 No (negative)I won't be bored. It will not rain.
❓ QuestionWill you be excited?
💬 Short answersYes, I will. / No, I won't.

💡 'll = will  •  won't = will not
